Penny Rounding

Automatically rounds cash totals to the nearest $0.05 — no more pennies, no more exact change hassle.

Available on the Clover App Market

How it works

  1. Tap the floating "Round for Cash" button while in Clover Register
  2. Review the original total, adjustment, and rounded total
  3. Tap Apply — the order is updated instantly
  4. Collect cash at the rounded amount using the standard Cash tender

Features

Smart rounding

Rounds up or down to the nearest $0.05 (max ±$0.02 adjustment) so cash drawers stay simple.

Flexible direction

Choose Auto, Always Up, or Always Down rounding to match your store's policy.

Customizable label

The adjustment line shown on the order can be relabeled to fit your receipt style.

Works everywhere

Runs as a floating button over all apps on supported Clover devices — no workflow changes needed.
