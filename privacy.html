Privacy Policy

Penny Rounding — an app for Clover

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Thomas Bailey ("we," "us," or "our"), the developer of Penny Rounding, handles information in connection with the Penny Rounding app for Clover point-of-sale devices ("the App"). This Privacy Policy is not a substitute for any privacy policy a Merchant may be required to provide to its own customers or personnel. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Access

The App requests the following permissions through the Clover platform, solely to provide its core rounding functionality:

  • Orders (Read & Write): to read the current open order's total and apply a rounding discount or line item adjustment when you tap "Round for Cash."
  • Merchant (Read only): to identify your merchant account and authenticate API requests with Clover.

The App does not request or access Customers, Employees, or Inventory data.

How We Use Information

Order and merchant information is used in real time, on-device and via direct calls to Clover's API, only to calculate and apply the cash-rounding adjustment you request. We do not use this information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the rounding feature.

Data Storage & Retention

The App does not maintain its own external database of your transactions. Order data is read from and written back to Clover's systems via the Clover API. App settings (such as your preferred rounding direction and adjustment label) are stored locally on the device using standard Android preferences and are not transmitted to us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share order, merchant, or transaction information with third parties. Information is exchanged only with Clover's own platform as required for the App to function.

Security

All communication with Clover's API uses Clover's standard authentication (CloverAuth) and HTTPS. We do not store payment card data, and the App never directly processes payments — all cash tendering is handled through Clover's standard Cash tender flow.

Children's Privacy

The App is a business tool intended for use by merchants and staff at the point of sale. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, the CCPA gives you the right to request the categories of personal information we have collected, the sources of that information, and the categories of third parties (if any) with whom it has been shared. As described above, the App only accesses Order and Merchant account information through the Clover platform, solely to perform the rounding calculation — we do not collect Customer, Employee, or Inventory data, and we do not sell or share personal information with third parties for any purpose. You can request access to or deletion of the limited information we hold by contacting us at the email below; we will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

Merchants Located in Europe or the UK

For Merchants located in Europe or the UK, Thomas Bailey acts as a data processor on behalf of the Merchant, who is the data controller of any personal data processed through the App. Clover also acts as a controller in some circumstances; Clover's own privacy notice is available at clover.com/privacy-policy. The App does not knowingly transfer personal data outside the Merchant's region beyond what is necessary to communicate with the Clover API. Data subjects in Europe or the UK with questions about our processing may contact us using the details below, or direct controller-related requests to the Merchant.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App or applicable law. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.
